About Us - Paddle Naija

Paddle Naija brings the thrill of white water rafting to Lagos, Nigeria, offering tourists and locals a fun and safe way to explore the beauty of our rivers.

We believe in courage, togetherness, and protecting nature while creating unforgettable adventures. Every ride is a chance to connect, discover, and enjoy the vibrant energy of Nigeria.

Paddle Bold, Paddle Naija.

History

Paddle Naija was born in the heart of Lagos, where life is loud, fast, and full of hustle.

In a city known for its energy, uncertainty, and endless movement, we found calm in the chaos—on the river.

A few friends, tired of the noise and stress, picked up paddles and chose joy. What started as a simple escape became a way to bring people together. The river didn’t care who you were or where you came from—it welcomed everyone.

Today, Paddle Naija stands as a symbol of unity, adventure, and the belief that even in the middle of life’s storms, we can choose to ride the current together.
