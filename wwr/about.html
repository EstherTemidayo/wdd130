Paddle Naija Adventure

Paddle Naija is all about adventure, connection, and good vibes on the water. We bring white water rafting to Lagos in a way that’s fun, safe, and totally unforgettable. Whether you're a first-timer or a thrill-chaser, Every ride here is a chance to breathe, laugh, and see the world from a fresh perspective.

History

Paddle Naija was born in the heart of Lagos, where life is loud, fast, and full of hustle. In a city known for its energy, uncertainty, and endless movement, we found calm in the chaos—on the river. A few friends, tired of the noise and stress, picked up paddles and chose joy. What started as a simple escape became a way to bring people together.
Today, Paddle Naija stands as a symbol of unity, adventure, and the belief that even in the middle of life’s storms, we can choose to ride the current together.
