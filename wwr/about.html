Paddle Naija Adventure

Paddle Naija is all about adventure, connection, and good vibes on the water. We bring white water rafting to Lagos in a way that’s fun, safe, and totally unforgettable. Whether you're a first-timer or a thrill-chaser, our river trips are designed to help you unwind, laugh, and experience nature like never before.

We believe in courage, the kind that shows up when you try something new. We believe in togetherness, because paddling is better with people by your side and we believe in protecting the rivers we ride.

Every trip is a chance to breathe, bond, and enjoy the wild beauty of Nigeria from a fresh perspective.

So if you're looking for something different, something real, something joyful—grab a paddle and join us. Every ride is a chance to breathe, laugh, and see Nigeria from a fresh perspective. Paddle Bold, Paddle Naija.

History

Paddle Naija was born in the heart of Lagos, where life is loud, fast, and full of hustle. In a city known for its energy, uncertainty, and endless movement, we found calm in the chaos—on the river. A few friends, tired of the noise and stress, picked up paddles and chose joy. What started as a simple escape became a way to bring people together. The river didn’t care who you were or where you came from—it welcomed everyone. Today, Paddle Naija stands as a symbol of unity, adventure, and the belief that even in the middle of life’s storms, we can choose to ride the current together.
